Our Frequent Flyers Program

Here at Air Zephyr, our Frequent Flyers Program is based on a point system, each hour you fly with us is 1,000 points (16 points per minute) into your account. Your point count resets annually on January 3rd, so be sure to get in as many flights as you can before then!


Troposphere Benefits: (Minimum Point Count: 10,000):
Half off any Air Zephyr In-flight Meal


Stratosphere Benefits: (Minimum Point Count: 25,000):
One Fourth off your hotel stay*


Mesosphere Benefits: (Minimum Point Count: 40,000):
TSA Pre-Check on the next flight of your choice
Half-off any Air Zephyr licensed item


Thermosphere Benefits: (Minimum Point Count: 55,000):
Admission into the Admiral’s Club at select airports
TSA Pre-Check on your next 3 flights of your choice


Exosphere Benefits: (Minimum Point Count: 70,000):
Free membership into the Zephyr Gold Plan**
Free item from any store at select airports
Half off your hotel stay*

Each flight also has to go smoothly, in the sense that you did not commit any crimes or cause any trouble, if this does happen, you will not receive any points for the current flight, nor the 2 after that, no matter the length (In fact, you may just be banned from the airline, depending on what you did).
*As long as the hotel you stay with is a partner of Air Zephyr (Check hotel website, or ask hotel itself)
**First Class or Coach, your choice, kosher in-flight meals, and Admiral’s Club admission
